Social Media Manager, IHRD Tharang ( National Level Tech Fest )

Description


I am thrilled to take a moment to share with you the incredible journey I've had as the Social Media Manager for the prestigious national-level techfest, IHRD Tharang. It has been a transformative experience filled with learning, growth, and exciting connections.

During my tenure, I had the privilege of managing the social media presence of IHRD Tharang, a platform that not only celebrates technology and innovation but also fosters a community of like-minded individuals.

Here are some key highlights of my role

Celebrity Connections:
Working on the social media strategy allowed me to establish meaningful connections with celebrities in the tech and innovation space. This not only added prestige to our events but also created exciting opportunities for collaboration and engagement.

Design Revitalization :
I took charge of redesigning and enhancing the visual appeal of our social media posts. This not only made our content more engaging but also contributed to a cohesive and attractive online presence for IHRD Tharang.

Event Updates and Networking :
Managing posters and updates for upcoming events gave me real-time insights into the latest happenings. It also provided a fantastic opportunity to connect with students from various parts of India and around the world, fostering a global network of tech enthusiasts.

Digital Marketing Expertise :
The role allowed me to delve deep into the world of digital marketing. From mastering the art of using hashtags strategically to exploring the realm of paid promotions, I contributed to enhancing the reach and impact of IHRD Tharang's online presence.

Global Reach :
Through concerted efforts in digital marketing, we were able to extend the reach of our social media accounts, connecting with students and tech enthusiasts not only across India but also from various corners of the globe.

As I move forward, I carry with me not just the technical skills gained but also a network of amazing individuals passionate about technology. I am immensely grateful for the support and collaboration of the entire IHRD Tharang team.
