President, PRODDEC CEC

Description


It is with immense pleasure and gratitude that I announce my recent promotion to the esteemed position of President of PRODDEC (Product Design and Development Center) at College Of Engineering Chengannur.

Having served as the PRO in PRODDEC CEC, this journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. I have been privileged to work alongside a team of talented individuals dedicated to innovation and excellence. The encouragement, support, and collaborative spirit within PRODDEC have been instrumental in our collective success.

I am sincerely thankful for the trust placed in me by the PRODDEC leadership, which has led to this remarkable opportunity. This promotion is a reflection of the hard work, commitment, and passion that each member of PRODDEC brings to the table.

As the new President of PRODDEC, I am excited about the challenges and responsibilities that come with this role. My vision is to further elevate PRODDEC's standing as a hub for innovation, creativity, and impactful product development within the College community and beyond.

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to every member of PRODDEC for their dedication and collaborative efforts. Together, we have built a culture of excellence, and I am confident that, as a team, we will achieve even greater milestones in the future.
