Class Representative CSE C Batch CEC

November 2020 - October 2023

Class Representative CSE C Battc CEC (2020 - 2023)

Description


I am delighted to reflect on an incredible chapter of my academic journey—a journey that began with my election as the Class Representative for the C batch (2020 admission batch) and continued successfully for three consecutive years.

Leadership as a First Journey :
Being entrusted with the responsibility of leading my peers was not just an honor but an invaluable learning experience. It marked the initiation of my leadership journey and provided me with immense opportunities for growth.

Conflict Resolution and Decision-Making :
In the role of a Class Representative, I learned the art of conflict resolution—how to unite conflicting groups, consider diverse perspectives, and make decisions that fostered unity without giving rise to new conflicts. This experience gave me the strength to take standalone decisions in challenging situations.

Effective Communication :
Learning to communicate effectively with diverse individuals became a crucial aspect of my role. It allowed me to connect with students from various backgrounds, understanding their concerns, and ensuring that everyone felt heard and valued.

Event Hosting and Initiatives :
As a representative, I took the initiative to host various events that brought our class together and initiated projects for the benefit of both students and the college community.

Issue Resolution and Retreat for Opportunity:
Enquiring about issues from my fellow students and resolving them became a part of my routine. The positive feedback from my peers motivated me to stand as the Class Representative for three consecutive terms. However, I voluntarily retreated from my post, recognizing the importance of providing opportunities for new leaders to emerge.

Impact Beyond the Classroom :
The experiences gained from my role as a Class Representative propelled me to attain top positions in two leading forums within the college. Additionally, I played a key role in a national-level tech fest, contributing to its success.

Recognition and Connections :
The journey made me a recognizable figure, and I became known as "Anandhu, the Class Rep of C Batch." This recognition extended beyond our college as I developed connections with representatives from other classes within our institution and even from other colleges.

I am immensely grateful for the trust and support of my peers, and I am excited about the continued growth and opportunities that lie ahead in my leadership journey.
