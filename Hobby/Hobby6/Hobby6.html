Situated in a separate room within my old home, this studio is a testament to personal expression and functionality. Let's delve into the details of its design, components, and the unique blend of aesthetics that make it a truly immersive space.

Artistic Appearance

The studio's aesthetic journey begins with a meticulous paint job. The entire room, along with half of the floor, is adorned in a striking black hue, creating a dramatic backdrop that sets the stage for creativity. A conscious blend of black and white elements contributes to a visually appealing and balanced environment.

Custom Sound System and Holder Setup

The auditory experience is elevated by a self-made 100-watt sound system, delivering crisp and immersive audio. A strategically positioned holder for the computer and speakers ensures an organized and efficient setup, contributing to a seamless recording experience.

Personal Touches

Infusing personal touches, I adorned the studio with two captivating astronaut-holding-guitar photos, creating a cosmic connection between music and the cosmos. On the opposite side, motivational boxing quotes serve as inspiration for intense workout sessions and video recordings.

Self-Made Tripod and Fan

A self-made tripod stands as a testament to hands-on craftsmanship, providing stable support for cameras during video shoots. The addition of a self-made fan operating on a 12-volt adapter introduces a quiet breeze, ensuring a comfortable, distraction-free and noise-free environment for recording.

Isolation for Optimal Sound

To maintain the studio's focus on audio quality, it is thoughtfully isolated from nearby homes. This isolation guarantees a quiet space, ideal for capturing the nuances of guitar melodies, drum beats, and the intensity of boxing workouts.
