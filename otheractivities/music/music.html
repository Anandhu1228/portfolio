Music creation and reinterpreting

My lifelong passion for music is evident in my portfolio. I've learned the guitar, crafted my own drum set, practiced drums, played the Android keyboard app Perfect Piano, and taken up the flute. My musical journey began with the guitar, expanded to Perfect Piano, continued with my self-made drums, and eventually led to my exploration of the flute.

Music has played a pivotal role in my life, serving as a source of hope, mental healing, stress reduction, pride, and physical well-being. Despite not singing or having knowledge of music theory, music is an integral part of who I am. An intense desire to explore more instruments has led me to plan the purchase of a keyboard and a violin.

I've also ventured into creating my own music, refactoring a song into my version for meditation purposes. Additionally, I dream of inventing a new musical instrument. I've covered various English, Hindi, Malayalam, and Tamil songs, and collaborated with friends to create fun music.

In the process, I've acquired skills in videography, video editing, and audio editing to enhance my music creation. Music will always be a constant in my life, and I firmly believe that combining art and science will contribute to creating a better world.
