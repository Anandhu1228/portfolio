Boxing

Self trained from youtube

Once I began my fitness journey, I developed a keen interest in acquiring boxing skills for self-defense. Having already learned karate in school, transitioning to boxing practice was relatively seamless. I turned to YouTube and extensively researched various videos to create an effective training routine, eventually discovering numerous helpful resources.

Initially, I worked out with a homemade punching bag before investing in a real punching bag and a pair of gloves. I even crafted my own double end bag. Starting with boxing drills, I gradually progressed to kickboxing over time.
