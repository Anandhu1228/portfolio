Flutist

Self learned from youtube

I have always harbored a deep passion for music, a fact clearly reflected in my portfolio. I've learned to play the guitar, crafted my own drum set, honed my drumming skills, and plays the Android keyboard app Perfect Piano. The constant desire to explore new instruments led me to discover the flute, drawn to its serene sound and recognized mental health benefits.

After thorough research, I invested in a series of high-quality flutes and scoured YouTube for guidance on where to begin and continue. Armed with those knowledge, I embarked on my flute-playing journey and have already managed to play two songs. While I currently allocate less time to the flute compared to other instruments, I am committed to dedicating more time to it after completing my academic pursuits. My goal is to achieve fluency in playing the flute by the age of 28.
