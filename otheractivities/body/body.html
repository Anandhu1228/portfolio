Body Building

Self trained from youtube

During my 8th, 9th, and 10th-grade years, I held the title of the shortest and lightest student in my class. Consequently, I harbored dreams of achieving a robust physique. It wasn't until I stepped into 11th grade when i started gaining body mass and height that I decided it was the opportune moment to embark on a journey to gain muscle mass.

I turned to YouTube for guidance, initiating my training regimen with improvised equipment like bricks, water-filled bottles, push-ups, pull-ups, cycling, and running. Resourcefully, I fashioned dumbbells using cement and steel pipes. Eventually, my parents supported my fitness aspirations by acquiring a 40 kg weight set, dumbbells, a barbell, and a bench for essential training.

Consistently, I dedicated myself to workouts, witnessing my body undergo positive transformations. By the time I entered college, I proudly stood out as the individual with commendable muscle mass and a well-defined physique in my class.
